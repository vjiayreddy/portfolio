## Overview

Project Alpha is a full-featured e-commerce platform designed for scalability and performance. Built with Next.js App Router and Payload CMS, it handles product catalog management, secure checkout, and order fulfillment.

## Challenge

The client needed a modern storefront that could handle high traffic during sales events while maintaining a seamless user experience.

## Solution

We architected a headless CMS approach with Payload CMS for content management and Next.js for the frontend, integrated with Stripe for payments.

## Results

The platform launched successfully and now serves thousands of daily users with excellent performance metrics.
