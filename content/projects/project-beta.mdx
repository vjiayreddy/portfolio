## Overview

Project Beta is a WhatsApp CRM system that automates customer communication workflows for businesses.

## Challenge

Manual customer support was overwhelming the team, leading to delayed responses and lost sales opportunities.

## Solution

Built a Next.js dashboard integrated with WhatsApp Business API and Supabase for real-time message tracking and automated responses.

## Results

Customer satisfaction scores improved significantly while support team workload decreased by 60%.
