## Overview

Project Gamma is a point-of-sale and inventory management system built for a multi-location tire retail business.

## Challenge

The business was using disconnected spreadsheets and legacy software, causing inventory discrepancies and slow checkout.

## Solution

Developed a unified Next.js application with MongoDB for real-time inventory sync across all store locations.

## Results

Inventory accuracy improved to 99% and staff training time was cut in half thanks to an intuitive UI.
